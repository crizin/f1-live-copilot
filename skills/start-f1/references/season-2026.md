## 2026 Regulation Changes

### Power Unit (major overhaul)
- Electric power **tripled**: 120kW → 350kW (~50% of total power is electric)
- MGU-H removed → simpler, cheaper PU architecture
- 100% sustainable fuel mandatory
- New manufacturers: Audi (own PU), Red Bull-Ford (own PU)

### Chassis
- Smaller, lighter cars (significant downsize from 2022-2025 era)
- Active aerodynamics introduced (adjustable aero surfaces)
- Lower minimum weight

### New Team
- **Cadillac** (GM) joins as 11th team — first new constructor since Haas (2016)
  - Ferrari customer engine
  - Bottas + Perez driver lineup
